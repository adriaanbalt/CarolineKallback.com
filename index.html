Hej! I am a Swedish video editor based in NYC with a passion for visual storytelling and the feelings that it can evoke. I am a happy green card holder who loves the city but am equally excited to travel. My background as a dancer and fashion model influences my understanding for movements, tone and rhythm. Rhythm is not only reflected in my work but also in my efforts to create the best workflow possible within a team. I constantly look for new ways to improve my efforts to create intriguing visuals. The journey never ends!
